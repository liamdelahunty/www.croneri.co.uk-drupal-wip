Synopsis: 

This webinar will help corporates and firms understand why The Economic Crime and Corporate Transparency Act 2023 has been introduced. We will explore Companies House changes coming into force during 2024, their implications, the upcoming filing requirements for small and micro companies, and the proposed new thresholds of the company size classification limits. 

We will share the resources available on the Croner-i Navigate research and learning platforms that provide further guidance. 

  

Key learning points: 

·        What is ECCTA, and why has it been introduced; 

·        The key Companies House changes that came into force on 4 March 2024 and their impact on companies; 

·        Proposed changes to the filing requirements of small and micro companies; 

·        Introduction to the new offence of failure to prevent fraud; and 

·        New thresholds to the Company size classification limits.
